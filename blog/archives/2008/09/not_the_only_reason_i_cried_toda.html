Not the Only Reason I Cried Today

<riiiing>

"Hi Mom!"

"Hi buddy! How did you know...?"

"I missed you when you were there and I was here!"

"Aw, I missed you, too! Did you have a good day?"

"Yes! And how was YOUR day?"

"Aww, well, I'm at a conference..."

"Hooray! I LOVE conferences!"

"Oh! Ha ha! Well..."

"I'm just kidding. Hee!"

"Right, of course. Well, I met lots of new people today..."

"Yaaaay!"

"Yes, it was good, meeting new people. That's hard sometimes."

"Yeah."

"I wish I was there to snuggle you."

"I wish you were here to snuggle me, too." [To his dad: "Do we have time? OK. OK."] "Mom, I have to go, but Daddy's going to talk to you."

"OK, thanks buddy, I love you."
