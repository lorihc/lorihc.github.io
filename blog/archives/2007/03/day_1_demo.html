Day 1: Demo

Al came through with some cameraphone photos of the Day 1 work, which consisted entirely of demo. I don't know why I'm surprised that they managed to strip most of the bathroom (save for the tub) to the studs in such a short time, but I am.


The view from the doorway. The black bit on the floor is where the toilet goes; it'll be coming back, because it's relatively new.


The debris from the vanity and wall removal, stacked in the tub.


Where the too-low vanity once stood. Although there are hookups for two sinks, we only had one (on the left); the new design will also incorporate only one sink, in the center.
