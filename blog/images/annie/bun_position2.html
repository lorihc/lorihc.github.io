Bun Position (during the kitchen remodel). I don't remember what the origin of "bun position" was; it could have been that she looked like a dinner roll to me, or that the way she folded her paws under reminded me of the namesake hairdo. From bun position sprung two other similar positions, which I don't have photos of: Modified Bun, where she sort of tips over sideways after being in bun position, and Cinnamon Bun, which is the traditional croissant-shaped pose that cats tend to sleep in.
